Moussaka

Go back


Description

Moussaka is a rich, flavorful Greek casserole that layers eggplant with a spiced meat filling, topped with a creamy bechamel sauce. This dish is often compared to lasagna but has distinct Mediterranean flavors. It's a hearty, comforting dish, perfect for family dinners or special occasions.

Ingredients

  • Eggplants
  • Olive oil
  • Ground meat (lamb or beef)
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Tomato paste
  • Red wine
  • Cinnamon, allspice, and nutmeg
  • Bechamel sauce ingredients: butter, flour, milk, and cheese
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Prepare Eggplants: Slice and salt eggplants, then fry or bake until golden.
  2. Make Meat Filling: Cook onions, garlic, and ground meat. Add spices, tomato paste, and wine; simmer.
  3. Prepare Bechamel Sauce: Melt butter, stir in flour, gradually add milk, and cook until thickened. Add cheese and season.
  4. Assemble Moussaka: Layer eggplant slices and meat filling in a baking dish. Cover with bechamel sauce.
  5. Bake: Cook in a preheated oven until the top is golden and bubbly.
