Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Go back


Description

A grilled cheese sandwich is a classic, comforting dish known for its simplicity and deliciousness. It typically involves melting cheese between slices of buttered bread, grilled to golden perfection. This dish is versatile and can be customized with different types of cheeses and added ingredients like tomatoes, bacon, or herbs.

Ingredients

  • Bread slices
  • Cheese (like cheddar, mozarella, American)
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Preparation: Butter one side of each bread slice.
  2. Assemble: Place cheese between the unbuttered sides of the bread.
  3. Grill: Cook the sandwich in a pan over medium heat until golden brown on both sides and the cheese is melted.
  4. Resting: Let the steak rest for a few minutes after cooking for juciness.
  5. Serve: Cut diagonally and serve warm.
